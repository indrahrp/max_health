## Proposed Mechanism (from transcripts)

The most cited mechanism across cases is **glucose toxicity / blood sugar normalization**:

- Diabetics losing vision from retinal damage due to hyperglycemia
- When A1C drops → retinal blood flow improves → vision stabilizes or recovers
- Dry eye resolves as inflammation drops
- Floaters and intraocular pressure may respond to reduced systemic inflammation

This aligns with known diabetic retinopathy and inflammatory eye disease pathways — carnivore's primary mechanism for eye improvement appears to be **lowering chronic glucose load + systemic inflammation**.
