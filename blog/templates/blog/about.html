Hi, I'm Indra Choki

Health Writer & Carnivore Diet Advocate

Passionate about reversing chronic disease through nutrition

I'm a health writer on a mission to share the transformative power of the carnivore diet for preventing and reversing chronic disease. My writing bridges personal experience with emerging nutritional science.

After struggling with my own health challenges, I discovered the animal-based diet and experienced results that conventional medicine couldn't provide. That journey sparked a deep curiosity — and eventually a commitment to helping others find the same clarity.

I believe that much of modern chronic illness is driven by what we eat, and that returning to ancestral, animal-based nutrition is one of the most powerful tools we have.
